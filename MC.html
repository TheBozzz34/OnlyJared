ArcticForest is a vanilla survival server with a friendly community and atmosphere about it. Sporting a relatively smaller-than-average playerbase, you will find many great places to build and set up your base or home. The world was originally made during the Alpha but, sadly, most of the Alpha chunks had to be deleted due to causing many corruption problems. We have great staff members who are always who are kind and happy to help. All in all ArcticForest is an extremely welcoming server for players of all types!
